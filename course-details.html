BSc-CSIT

About BSc-CSIT

This program is considered as the most attractive among the new programs offered by Tribhuvan University. BSc.CSIT is a four years (eight semester) program. This course is designed minimum of 126 credit hours. Among the core computer Science 75 credit hours, system and programming electives 15 credit hours, Natural Science 12 credit hours, Management 6 credit hours, Math's 6 credit hours, Technical Writing Technical Writing 3 hours and ject and internship includes 9 credit hours.

Software and Programming

Unlike other courses CSIT mainly focus on practical and resource development that could earn as early as the graduation completes. Courses are designed to take full advantage for placements. Our trainings on software development adds beauty on the course. The Programming course like c/c++ , Java , dot net and our web and software development adds beauty on the course. The Progrmming courses like c/c++ , java, dot net and our web and software development trainings offered in every semesters makes our students ahead in any competitions.

Research and Development

As this course is not just IT course it consist maximum of the courses that take you to the research. After graduation CSIT students take two paths one on Research after further continuation on Masters and PHd and another to the highly skilled jobs in the industry.

MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS FOR ENROLLMENT IN NEPATHYA COLLEGE TO STUDY BSc.CSIT

♦ 10 +2 graduate from Science Background
♦ Pass entrance examination conducted by TU from any college.

♦ BrainDigit KTM
♦ Infoxit BTL
♦ Innovative Techno Consultant BTL
♦ PowerCode IT Solutions BTL
